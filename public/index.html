About US

We, at Hound Electric believe that it's time to rethink the way we commute and look at the automotive sector as a whole. Internal Combustion engines are but a technology of the past,vestiges of great minds a century old. Today, we are determined to change it. The answer lies with the little explored but immensely powerful science of electricity. Built with the best intentions, electric machines of today's market without intent spoil the reputation of electric vehicles; slow and powerless, they besmirch the good name of electric bikes and undermine what an electric vehicle can do.

We, here at Hound Electric are talking about an entirely different breed, something every man would want to ride; some thing, that makes the heart pound harder. Something so revolutionary and versatile that anyone can use anywhere.

Achievements

`Best 100 Start-ups of Startup Village

Listed among `Best 100 Start-ups' among more than 800 start-ups incubated at Startup Village, Kochi. They were featured in the 'SV Catalouge', which showcases the Best 100 Startups and was referred to as the "Most Innovative Startup in Startup Village".

Best Innovative Idea Award - YES 2015

Hound electric was awarded the Most Innovative Idea Award by the Kerala State Government at the Young Entrepreneurs Summit(YES) 2015 held at Kochi, India. YES is a Government of Kerala initiative to foster Entrepreneurship among the youth as a part of Startup Mission by the Kerala State Industrial Development Corporation.

Best Exhibitor Award - YES 2014

Hound electric received the Best Exhibit Award at the Young Entrepreneurs Summit 2014 held at Kochi, India.
